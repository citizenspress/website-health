Markham Stouffville Hospital Clerical Employees Information Meeting (Aug 15, 2017)

CUPE Information Meeting To All Markham Stouffville Hospital Clerical Employees You are invited to attend an information session to find out more about CUPE’s Application under the Public Sector Labour Relation Transition Act (PSLRTA) to the Ontario Labour Relations Board and what that means for non union employees at MSH. Information as well on how to join CUPE and the protection CUPE can provide during this process will also be available.

Markham Stouffville Hospital Clerical Employees Information Meeting (Aug 15, 2017)

CUPE Information Meeting

To All Markham Stouffville Hospital Clerical Employees

You are invited to attend an information session to find out more about CUPE’s Application under the Public Sector Labour Relation Transition Act (PSLRTA) to the Ontario Labour Relations Board and what that means for non union employees at MSH. Information as well on how to join CUPE and the protection CUPE can provide during this process will also be available. The information meeting is being held:

Date:

TUESDAY, AUGUST 15, 2017

Where:

Markham District Veterans Association
7 Washington Street, Markham
(behind Tim Horton’s)

Time:

Drop in anytime between 4:00 p.m. – 8:00 p.m.

Please come out and join us; have your questions and concerns answered and encourage your colleagues to join you. You can also find out more about CUPE and the PSLRTA process at cupehospitals.ca

If you would like to speak to someone over the phone, please call Deb Oldfield at 905-739-3999 Ext. 276 or email ontarioorganizer@cupe.ca.

We look forward to seeing you there!

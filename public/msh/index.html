Clerical employees at Markham Stouffville Hospital are joining CUPE

Back in January 2004, the Cottage Hospital Uxbridge was placed under the jurisdiction of Markham Stouffville Hospital. At that time, and up until recently, the work performed at both sites has remained separate. However, recently there have been some changes at the Uxbridge location and work that was normally performed by employees at the Uxbridge site has been moved to the Markham Stouffville site. These employees are members of CUPE Local 6364.

As a result of this ‘intermingling’ of work, CUPE will be filing a PSLRTA Application. This application could affect employees at Markham Stouffville Hospital including non-union clerical employees. Please be assured that this Application will in no way result in job losses or unionized workers taking non-union employee positions. Job loss can only result in the Hospital deciding to restructure or cutback positions.

CUPE is currently organizing the non union clerical at the Markham Stouffville location so that these employees can enjoy the same rights and benefits as the members of Local 6364 including job security, job posting language, a fair process for shift scheduling and schedules posted weeks in advance for a 6 week period.

Find out more about how CUPE can help. Contact us at ontarioorganizer@cupe.ca or at 1-877-533-2873.

Your right to join a union is protected by law in Ontario.

In Ontario the OLRA protects the rights of workers to join a Union. Sections 70 and 72 of the OLRA prohibit employers from interfering with an employee’s right to join a union and ensures that employees are free from coercion, intimidation, threats, promises or undue influence, including reprisals for their support and involvement with the union. If the employer violates those rights CUPE will file an Unfair Labour Practice application with the Labour Board. Read More…
